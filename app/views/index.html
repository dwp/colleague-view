Colleague View

Colleague view portal allows agents to access multiple benefits of a citizen.

The service is used for accessing the following things for citizen:

  • Answer basic customer queries about benefits they receive
  • Log the details of contact with a customer
  • Check previous contact the customer has had with the DWP
  • Access a customer's personal details
