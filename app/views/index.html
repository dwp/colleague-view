Colleague View

Colleague View will make it simple for agents to resolve simple enquiries across multiple benefit lines by providing a single view of the customer and their benefits in one place. This will enable the transformation of our services to a tiered operating model, separating our colleagues and our customers from the complexity of our services.

Objectives:

  • consistency of experience
  • improved safeguarding
  • minimised use of unstructured data
  • providing enough information to an agent, within a simple, intuitive user interface.

Prototype based on GDS standards and user testing rounds.
