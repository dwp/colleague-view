Colleague View prototypes

The aim of the Colleague View minimum viable product (MVP) is give customers’ benefit information to agents answering the telephones, so that they can answer common questions.

The users that we are focusing on for MVP are agents that work on Employment and Support Allowance (ESA).

The scope of the MVP is to meet agents’ needs for:

  • answering questions about next payment dates and amounts
  • viewing a customer’s personal details
  • adding details about a call with a customer
  • viewing details about previous calls with a customer

Research goal

To test two prototypes that show a different call-recording screen flow each, to identify the following points in why we tested below

What we tested

Payment information, deductions, contact recording, finding past records

Why we tested

To test two prototypes that show a different call-recording flow, to identify:

  • Anywhere our design no longer supports what agents need to do, given changes to the prototype. This is for a main user task of payment-related queries.
  • Where our design does and does not support frontline colleagues making a record of a call, using our newly designed call-recording function.
  • Where our design does and does not support frontline colleagues finding a past record.
  • Where our design meets accessibility needs

Sprint 39 Completed
March 2024: Business release

Prototype MVP 1.0

This prototype builds on the UR 7 option 2 journey to include changes required based on feedback from Service Modernisation. This design allows agents to access a customer's benefit information if they had a different type of contact with the user other than a phone call. There is also an option to only view a customer’s benefit information - this is for Quality Checkers and team leads.

These additional screens have not been tested with users. The Colleague View team and Service Modernisation are aware of the risks of releasing a service with no user research on particular journeys.
